Skill System Overview

Geometric Trigger-Based Combo System
Skills don't activate individually — they trigger based on placement geometry on your board. Adjacency, distance, and angle determine combos. 1.38 × 10¹¹² possible builds.

Skill Types

  • Passive (Always-On) — Trigger regardless of flip outcome. Best for consistent scaling.
  • On-Win — High variance, snowball potential.
  • On-Lose — Comeback/insurance builds.
  • Node Trigger — Trigger 100% of nodes regardless of win/loss. Top-tier strategy.
