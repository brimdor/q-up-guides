What Is Q-UP?

Q-UP is a coin-flipping eSport where every round is truly 50/50 — no reaction time, no smurfs, no skill gap. You equip items and skills before each flip, and the best build determines who wins and who loses. Think of it as deckbuilding meets idle incremental where the RNG is the whole game.

Early Game (Novice → Gold)

  1. Pick one hero and stick with it — Skill tree progression is hero-specific. Spreading points across heroes early delays your first build.
  2. Buy cheap Common/Uncommon items — Focus on boosting QMult. Even small multipliers compound over time.
  3. Learn Recycling immediately — Don't sell items for base gold. Recycle them in sets for Gems. Gems fuel permanent upgrades.
  4. Target the Target Set — Every recycling session highlights one set that gives double. Complete it to permanently increase that set's future bonus.
  5. Don't chase rarity — A fully-activated Uncommon set outperforms a poorly-assembled Rare set.

Mid Game (Gold → High Rank)

  • Experiment with 2–3 heroes to understand different geometric trigger patterns.
  • Start prioritizing Branch stacking (Mech, Magic, Material) — "Timeline Saturated" is a reliable +173%.
  • Begin collecting Duel items for Top Gear and PVP sets.
  • Learn which items give bonus Gold — needed for "Luxary Goods" set (+200%).

End Game

  • Unlock Medic and attempt the Infinity QMult build if your tree is deep enough.
  • Collect Impossible rarity items for the +300% Anomaly set.
  • Optimize team compositions for 4v4.
✅ Quick Wins (Day 1)
  • Play 10 offline bot matches. You keep rewards. No risk.
  • Buy 6 Common items. Recycle as "Trash Collection" (+50%).
  • Buy 2 pairs of identical Uncommons. Recycle as "Two Pairs" (+202%).
  • Spend all starting Gems on QMult upgrades.
  • Read the in-game tutorial emails.
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Don't go into Solo-Q before trying offline. You'll lose rank and gold.
  • Don't hoard items — if you aren't equipping them, recycle them.
  • Don't ignore the Gear Recycler. It's the strongest gold efficiency tool.
